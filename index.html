Our Mission


Connect students to a safe buddy system when walking and traveling.

Feeling unsafe in a university area? Creating a travel room has never been easier!
There are lots of opportunities to find exactly the right travel group for you. Choose the place you want to visit, where you're visiting from, and write a quick note explaining the type of person you'd like to travel with or what you are intending to do while away.
A new room will quickly be created and other travellers can get the chance to join you, get in contact and discuss plans.
